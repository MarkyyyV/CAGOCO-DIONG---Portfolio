Our Projects

Cagoco's Project

Our project is an application which helps us and other plant lovers manage our home plant records through GreenHabit. We created this idea because after our meeting about this project we discuss about if we have a common hobby, after our little meeting we have which are planting and caring plants, but sometimes we forget when to water or check them because of a lot of assignments and staying in our gadgets all day. The application is capable of:

1. Adding a new plant to the list;
2. Viewing all plant records in an organized list.
3. Searching and viewing a specific plant's information;
4. Updating details such as watering schedule, sunlight needs, and growth status;
5. Deleting a plant record that is no longer active;
6. Sorting plants by name, date planted, or watering frequency;

Diong's Project

This is our final project for the course MS101 — Discrete Mathematics. For this project we created a simple solar‑powered mini fan: sunlight is captured by a mini solar panel (6 V, 1 W) that charges a rechargeable 3.7 V lithium‑ion battery, and the battery powers the DC motor (fan), demonstrating a practical, sustainable application of the concepts we studied.
